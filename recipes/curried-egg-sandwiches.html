Curried Egg Sandwiches

Delicious curried egg salad sandwiches guaranteed to fill you up.

Ingredients

  • 4 hard-cooked eggs, peeled and chopped
  • Â½ cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 8 slices bread

Steps

  1. Gather your ingredients.
  2. Mix together mayonnaise and curry powder in a bowl. Gently stir in eggs, then season to taste with salt and pepper.
  3. Evenly divide between 4 slices of bread, top with remaining 4 slices.
